EARTHQUAKE IN NEPAL

25th April, 2015

11:56:25 AM

The 7.8 Richter Scale Quake

  • Killed 9,000 and injured nearly 22,000 people
  • Triggered an avalanche on Mount Everest, killing 21
  • Out of 691 damaged historical sites, 131 were completely destroyed
  • Many houses were crumbled

Hundreds of Aftershocks

  • 17 days later, a second 7.3 magnitude quake hit
  • It caused landslides resulting in further damage and suffering
  • Many people became homeless

Two years later: 2017

  • The historic sites are still not reconstructed
  • Only 12% of 4.1 billion donation has been distributed
  • Silent Government
